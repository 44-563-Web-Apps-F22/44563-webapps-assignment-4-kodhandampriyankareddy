The Sorcerer

ACT II

Scene—Exterior of Sir Marmaduke's mansion by moonlight. All the peasantry are discovered asleep on the ground, as at the end of Act I.


Enter Mr. Wells, on tiptoe, followed by Alexis and Aline. Mr. Wells carries a dark lantern.

LADY S.

What is this fairy form I see before me?

MR. W.

Oh horrible!—She's going to adore me! This last catastrophe is overpowering!

LADY S.

Why do you glare at one with visage lowering? For pity's sake recoil not thus from me!

MR. W.

My lady leave me—this may never be! DUET—LADY SANGAZURE and MR. WELLS.
